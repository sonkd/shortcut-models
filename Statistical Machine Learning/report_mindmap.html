🟠 Moderate Issues
[M1] §4 (Inference) and §5 (Analysis) are structurally inverted The paper's stated purpose is "Evaluate the theoretical aspects" (Topic R14). Placing empirical §4 before theoretical §5 buries the main contribution. Recommended order: §4 Theoretical Analysis → §5 Empirical Validation, or merge as §4 Theory + §5 Experiment.
[M2] §1.3 Contributions (C1–C5) redundant with §6 Future Work (P1–P3) C5 in §1.3 says "we identify three concrete research gaps" — and then §6 lists P1/P2/P3 which are those same gaps. Either (a) remove C5 from §1.3 and just say "§6 identifies open problems," or (b) merge §1.3 C5 and §6 into a single clean list.
[M3] §3.2.4 Computational complexity O(T)→O(1) is oversimplified The O(1) claim applies only to 1-step inference. M-step shortcut is O(M), with M up to 128. More importantly, training complexity is O(log₂M · N) due to bootstrap levels — not mentioned. This section should be revised or removed.
[M4] §8.2 Reproducibility Checklist contains wrong reference "Pretrained weights from clip.load('ViT-B/32')" is CLIP, unrelated to this paper. Should reference the DiT-B checkpoint from the authors' Google Drive.
🟢 Minor Issues
[N1] Fig 4 placement Fig 4 shows the FID stats matrix visualization but appears mid-§4.2 between formula and "absolute vs relative FID" paragraph. It breaks reading flow — move to Appendix or after the stats structure paragraph ends.
[N2] FID curve caption (§4.4) Caption still says "diminishing returns from NFE≥16" — already corrected in conversation but may not be updated in draft. Log-linear trend, not diminishing returns.
[N3] Visual grid caption (§4.4) Same — "NFE=16 ≈ NFE=128" is overstated. Visible delta in hair texture and color. Caption updated in conversation but needs to be reflected in draft.
